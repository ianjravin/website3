welcome to Nouveta a payments company, easing payments to everyone.

We are a customer engagement & experience business, developing channels that businesses use to engage their customers & enabling payments across all these channels. Channels include SMS, USSD, app, Web & social media We operate a scalable, omni-channel payments platform that processes payments, manages devices & connects to various 3rd party systems & applications. We are an IBM business partner & provide services such as cognitive data analytics, predictive analytics, discovery through Watson(including chatbots) & API Management. what we do

Payments

We integrate various payments methods including mobile money (MPESA, Airtel Money & Equitel) & Card payments. We also integrate Bank transactions for direct-debit & will continue to integrate any new payment services.

Payment Channels

We enable our clients' customers to make payments across all touch points e.g USSD, SMS, Apps, Web, social media as well as physical touch points such as service centers & call centers. Our services are designed to make it easy for customers to pay their service providers

Conversation & discovery

We leverage on artificial intelligence & machine learning to develop chatbots, robots & intuitive solutions that innovate & personalize service delivery. We develop bots for social media, including Telegram & Watson & integrate payments solutions.

Integration, Dashboards & Reconcilliation

We integrate or platform with client systems to provide automated reconcilliation for payments made to financial & customer management systems. We provide dashboards that show payments & engagement analytics.

Our Products

Node

Node is our proprietary payments gateway that processes payments(mobile, cards & bank), manages devices & connects to various 3rd party systems & applications. Node is deployed as a platform as a service (PaaS) for several clients, together with dashboards & analytics.

PayME

PayMe is our business/merchant application that can be implemented either on Node or on client sytems. It sends request to pay to various mobile wallets, requiring the customer to only enter their PIN number. We implement this application across all customer touch points including USSD, SMS, app, web, social media & chatbots.

RevenueSure

RevenueSure is our revenue management platform that provides our clients with an end-to-end solution for management of revenues, revenue streams, revenue collection & revenue collection devices. The system provides detailed & intuitive analytics to enable our clients to track collections, devices & staff, & can be customized to track revenue locations & sites.

TicketSoko

TicketSoko is our ticketing platform that allows for 1-click purchase of tickets. it provides our clients with a management portal that allows them to create events & also to add events merchandise for sale. The platform allows for various ways to receive tickets for customers including SMS & PDF through email. It also provides a QR code validation application as well as detailed analytics to show even attendance & ticket sales.
